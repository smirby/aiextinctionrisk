Loss of Control: AI Existential Risk — Interactive Bowtie Assessment

A quantitative risk visualisation of pathways to loss of human control over advanced AI. Modelled using Monte Carlo simulation (n=1,000 trials) with triangular probability distributions across all barrier and threat parameters. Click any element in the diagram to explore its parameters and expert justifications.

Model by Michael G. Tyshenko, PhD, MPA — Risk Analyst, Risk Sciences International, Ottawa, Canada (risksciences.com). Chapter 11 data; interactive tool built from the original model workbook.

Data & Model: Michael G. Tyshenko, PhD, MPA — Risk Sciences International (risksciences.com). All probability estimates, triangular distribution ranges, expert source justifications and Monte Carlo parameterisation taken directly from the original model workbook (January 2026).
Methodology: Bowtie QRA with triangular distributions and Monte Carlo simulation (n=1,000). PFD = Probability of Failure on Demand. P(Top Event) = 1 − ∏i[1 − P(Ti) × ∏j PFD(TiPj)]. Expected Severity = ∑j [P(TE) × ∏k PFD(CjMk) × Sev(Cj)].
Key sources cited in model: Carlsmith (2021); Ord (2020, updated 2024); AI Impacts Expert Survey (2023); Gladstone AI (2024); RAND Corporation (2025); Hubinger et al. (2019).
