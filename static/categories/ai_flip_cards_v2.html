Interactive category cards

Flip the card to see who’s behind each label

Front: category, nickname, and short descriptor. Back: names only. Built to be tap-friendly on mobile, but still clean on desktop.

Note: These cards reproduce a set of provisional public categorizations. The labels are interpretive rather than definitive, and they are subject to revision as views change or become clearer.
