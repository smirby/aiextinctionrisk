title: "G20 AI Governance Comparison"
---

Chapter 4 of *Artificial Intelligence and Human Extinction Risk* includes a systematic comparison of AI governance frameworks across all G20 members. Each jurisdiction was coded against six analytical dimensions derived from the book's core distinctions: the Leiss framework (external control vs. self-control) and the tool-to-actor gradient.

The comparison produces four principal findings — among them, that no jurisdiction on earth has a regulatory framework containing provisions that would remain functional if an AI system behaved as an autonomous actor rather than a directed tool.
